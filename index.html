A Bit About Me

I am Dr. Vibha Shrivastava, a dedicated professor at Sagar Institute of Science and Technology in Bhopal.

As the head of the first-year department, I take great pride in guiding and nurturing the budding talents in our institution. My primary focus lies in the realm of chemistry, a subject I am deeply passionate about. Over the years, I have had the privilege of imparting knowledge and igniting curiosity in the minds of aspiring chemists. Beyond teaching, I am driven by a profound interest in research. Exploring the intricacies of this dynamic field is not only intellectually stimulating but also holds the promise of contributing to the broader scientific community.

This dual role as an educator and researcher fuels my commitment to fostering academic excellence and pushing the boundaries of knowledge in the world of chemistry.
